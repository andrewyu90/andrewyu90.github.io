Research #

Perception with Touch + Vision

High-resolution tactile sensing paired with visual scene understanding; simulation-to-real transfer; 3D-aware geometric priors.

Generative Video Models

Diffusion and adversarial generators for identity-preserving video editing and face swapping with temporal consistency.

Multimodal Fusion

Contrastive and probabilistic fusion of visual, tactile, and kinematic cues for dexterous manipulation and recognition.

Efficient & Real-Time

Model distillation, lightweight backbones, and streaming inference for deployment on robots and edge devices.
